Welcome to Social Cookbook

Social Cookbook enables you to store your recipes online, and it makes it easy to share your recipes with your friends and family and see the recipes your friends and family are cooking. When you add a recipe to your cookbook, your friends will also be able to see it, and they can clip the recipe into their own cookbooks. Likewise, you can add any of your friends' recipes to your own cookbook with a single click.

It looks like you are the first of your friends to join the site, so we don't have any recipes to suggest to you right now.

To get started, click the button below to add a recipe to your cookbook. It will be shared on your Facebook profile, and when your friends join the site, they will automatically see the recipe, and you can start sharing recipes with each other.

Happy cooking!
